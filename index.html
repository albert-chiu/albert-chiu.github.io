Welcome to my website! I'm a PhD student at Stanford's Department of Political Science. The ambition of my research is to help build a more credible regime of scientific discovery through the development of interpretable, accessible, and rigorous methods.

I graduated summa cum laude with a B.S. in Applied Mathematics, a B.A. in Political Science, and a minor in Economics from UC San Diego. Some of my work has appeared in The Journal of Politics. You can find a copy of my CV here.
