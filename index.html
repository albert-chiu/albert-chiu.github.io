Welcome to my website! I'm a PhD student at Stanford's Department of Political Science. The goal of my research is to produce, adapt, and apply statistical and machine learning methods for social scientific purposes. My substantive interests include global health and development, authoritarian and mixed regimes, and regime change.

I graduated summa cum laude with a B.S. in Applied Mathematics, a B.A. in Political Science, and a minor in Economics from UC San Diego. Some of my work has appeared in The Journal of Politics. You can find a copy of my CV here.
